# Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is **Aingweuy** policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application, **Aingweuy QR**.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Camera Permission
We require camera access to scan QR codes. Images scanned are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

### Storage Permission
We require storage access to save generated QR codes or barcodes to your device gallery.

### Location Permission
Required on some Android versions to discover and connect to Wi-Fi networks via QR codes. We do not track or store your physical location.

### Wi-Fi Connection
Required to allow the app to connect your device to Wi-Fi networks scanned from QR codes.

## 2. Usage of Information
We do not collect or share any personally identifiable information. All data processing related to QR scanning and generation happens locally on your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We may use third-party services like **Google AdMob** to display advertisements. These services may collect information used to identify you.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at **support@aing.blog**.
